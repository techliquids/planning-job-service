Dispute Resolution Process

  1. User raises dispute with details and proof
  2. Other party gets 48 hours to respond
  3. Admin reviews both sides and evidence
  4. Admin makes decision and implements resolution
  5. Loser can appeal within 7 days
  6. Final decision is binding

🔄 Job Execution Flow

Complete lifecycle of a job from posting to completion and payment.

Complete Job Lifecycle

  1. Job Posted: Employer posts a job with details and budget
  2. Job Listed: Job appears in search results for workers
  3. Proposals Sent: Interested workers send proposals with pricing
  4. Chat & Negotiation: Employer chats with workers to clarify
  5. Job Accepted: Employer accepts one proposal, payment initiated
  6. Payment Held: Amount held in secure escrow
  7. Worker Travels: Worker travels to job location with tracking
  8. Job Started: Worker arrives and checks in (GPS/OTP)
  9. Work in Progress: Worker performs service, employer can monitor
  10. Job Completed: Worker marks complete, uploads proof (photos/video)
  11. Employer Confirms: Employer verifies quality and confirms completion
  12. Payment Released: Amount released from escrow to wallets
  13. Commission Deducted: Automatic commission deduction applied
  14. Wallet Credited: Worker receives payment in wallet (net amount)
  15. Invoice Generated: Detailed invoice created with all details
  16. Rating & Review: Both parties can rate and review each other
✅ Success Criteria: Job completed on time with employer satisfaction and timely payment. Average job completion: 2-4 hours from posting to completion.
