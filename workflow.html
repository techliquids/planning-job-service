📋 Complete Job Booking Workflow

📌 Booking Flow Summary

The complete job booking process involves coordinated steps from both employer and worker, with parallel real-time updates and communications. The entire process typically takes 15-60 minutes from job posting to completion.

📝 Detailed Booking Steps

Step-by-Step Process

STEP 1: Employer Searches Workers

Employer opens the app and searches for available workers by service type and location

  • 📍 GPS location determines nearby workers
  • 🎯 Filters by ratings, distance, hourly rate
  • 💡 AI recommendations based on previous jobs
Estimated Time: 2-3 minutes

STEP 2: Select Worker & Create Job

Employer selects a worker and provides job details

  • ✍️ Detailed job description entered
  • 📸 Upload images/videos of the problem
  • 📅 Select date, time, and duration
  • 💰 View estimated cost
Estimated Time: 3-5 minutes

STEP 3: Payment Processing

Employer makes payment through preferred channel

  • 💳 Select payment method (UPI/Card/Wallet)
  • ✅ Payment authorized and confirmed
  • 📧 Invoice generated automatically
  • 🔔 Notification sent to worker
Estimated Time: 1-2 minutes

STEP 4: Worker Receives & Accepts

Worker gets notification and decides to accept/reject

  • 🔔 Push notification with 60-second timer
  • 👁️ Worker reviews job details, location, payment
  • ✅ Worker accepts or rejects job
  • 📍 GPS location shared with employer
Estimated Time: 1-2 minutes

STEP 5: Worker Arrives & Starts Job

Worker travels to location and begins work

  • 🗺️ GPS navigation to job location
  • 🟢 Employer receives ETA and live tracking
  • ▶️ Worker clicks "Start Job" button
  • ⏱️ Timer starts for billing
Estimated Time: 5-20 minutes (travel time)

STEP 6: Job Execution with Real-time Updates

Worker performs the service with continuous updates

  • 💬 In-app chat for coordination
  • 📸 Worker can share progress photos
  • ⏸️ Worker can pause/resume if needed
  • 🔄 Employer gets live updates
Estimated Time: 30 min - 8 hours (varies by job)

STEP 7: Job Completion & Work Proof

Worker completes job and submits proof

  • ✅ Click "Complete Job" button
  • 📸 Upload before-after photos/videos
  • 📍 GPS location tagged to images
  • 💵 Payment triggered to worker's wallet
Estimated Time: 2-3 minutes

STEP 8: Employer Verification & Payment

Employer reviews completed work

  • 📸 Review work photos and videos
  • ✅ Accept work or raise dispute
  • 💳 Final payment settled
  • 📊 Invoice downloaded
Estimated Time: 2-5 minutes

STEP 9: Rating & Review

Both parties rate and review each other

  • ⭐ Employer rates worker (1-5 stars)
  • 💬 Written feedback provided
  • ⭐ Worker rates employer
  • 📊 Ratings impact future bookings
Estimated Time: 2-3 minutes

STEP 10: Job Closed

Job completed successfully with all records saved

  • ✅ Job marked as completed
  • 💾 Complete record stored
  • 📊 Statistics updated
  • 🔄 Option to re-book same worker
Instant

⏱️ Total Time Summary

Quick Service Job (e.g., simple repair): 30 minutes - 2 hours from search to completion

Medium Service Job (e.g., plumbing): 2 - 8 hours from search to completion

Long Service Job (e.g., construction help): 8+ hours or multiple days

🔀 Decision Trees & Branch Scenarios

Job Acceptance Scenarios

✅ Worker ACCEPTS Job

→ Job status changes to "Accepted"

→ Worker GPS location shared with employer

→ Employer sees ETA

→ Job proceeds to execution phase

❌ Worker REJECTS Job

→ Job marked as "Rejected"

→ Payment held in employer's wallet

→ Job automatically offered to next highest-rated worker

→ Employer notified of rejection

→ Process repeats until acceptance

⏱️ Worker TIMEOUT (No response in 60 sec)

→ Job auto-rejected (no penalty to worker)

→ Offered to next worker immediately

→ Employer updated with new ETA

Job Completion Scenarios

✅ Employer ACCEPTS Work

→ Payment transferred to worker

→ Job marked as "Completed"

→ Rating prompt appears

→ Invoice finalized

🚨 Employer DISPUTES Work

→ Job status: "Dispute Raised"

→ Payment held in escrow

→ Admin investigation initiated

→ Evidence review (photos, messages, location)

→ Resolution: Refund or Full/Partial Payment

❌ Worker ABANDONS Job

→ Job status: "Abandoned"

→ Auto-refund to employer

→ Worker receives penalty

→ Job offered to next worker

1

Location Streaming

Worker's GPS location updated to employer every 5-10 seconds during job execution via WebSocket connection

2

Status Updates

Job status changes (accepted, started, completed) pushed instantly to both parties via Firebase notifications

3

In-app Chat

Bidirectional messaging with message persistence, read receipts, and typing indicators

4

Photo Uploads

Real-time photo sharing with GPS tagging and timestamp verification for work proof

⚠️ Error Handling & Edge Cases

📱 Network Disconnection

Scenario: Worker loses internet during job

Handling: GPS location cached locally, re-synced when connection restored. Job timer continues. No data loss.

💳 Payment Failure

Scenario: Payment gateway declines transaction

Handling: Employer notified immediately, job creation cancelled, retry option offered. No worker notification if payment fails.

🚫 Worker Cancel After Acceptance

Scenario: Worker accepts job but cancels before arrival

Handling: Job status reverted, payment refunded, job offered to next worker. Worker receives penalty on cancellation rate.

📍 GPS Spoofing Detection

Scenario: Worker claims to be at job location but isn't

Handling: System validates location consistency, cross-checks with photos. Suspicious behavior triggers admin review.

⏱️ Extended Job Duration

Scenario: Job takes longer than estimated

Handling: Worker can request extension, employer approves/rejects. Payment adjusted automatically based on hourly rate.
