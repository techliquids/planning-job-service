💰 Revenue Streams

🔧 Commission per Job

10-15%

Primary revenue source. Charged on the gross job amount paid to workers. Typically 10% in normal hours, 15% during surge pricing.

Example: ₹2,000 job → ₹200 commission (10%) in normal hours, ₹300 (15%) during peak hours

📈 Surge Pricing Premium

20-30%

During peak demand hours (7-9 AM, 5-7 PM), job prices increase. Platform gets additional 20-30% premium on top of normal commission.

Example: ₹2,000 job becomes ₹2,600 during surge (30% premium) → Platform earns ₹260 + ₹39 = ₹299

⭐ Featured Worker Listings

₹500-1,000/month

Workers can pay for premium placement, higher visibility in search results, and featured badge. Recurring subscription.

Target: 20% of active workers (1,000 workers) × ₹750 avg = ₹7.5 Lakhs/month

💎 Worker Premium Plan

₹300/month

Premium features: advance job requests, priority matching, analytics dashboard, reduced commission (8% instead of 10%)

Target: 15% adoption rate → ₹3-4 Lakhs/month additional revenue

🎁 Employer Subscription

₹500/month

Business plan for employers: bulk booking discounts, priority matching, API access, analytics, dedicated support

Target: 100 business subscribers × ₹500 = ₹5 Lakhs/month

💳 Payment Processing Fees

1-2.5%

Platform keeps portion of payment gateway fees. UPI: 0% (free), Card: 2.5%, Wallet transfers: 1%

Example: ₹2,000 card payment → Platform earns ₹50 processing fee
