📋 Complete Payment Process

1

Job Accepted & Payment Initiated

After worker accepts the job, employer initiates payment through the platform. Amount is captured and verified.

Amount Captured: Job budget specified during job posting + any additional charges
2

Payment Gateway Processing

Payment is processed through integrated payment gateway (Razorpay, Stripe, or local gateway). Transaction is authorized and verified.

Payment Methods Accepted: Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI, Digital Wallets, Bank Transfer
3

Amount Held in Escrow

Successfully paid amount is held in a secure escrow account. Funds are not released to the worker immediately. This protects both parties.

Escrow Duration: Until job completion + employer confirmation (typically 24-48 hours)
4

Worker Performs Job

Worker arrives at location, starts job, and completes assigned tasks with employer monitoring and real-time updates.

Tracking: GPS check-in → Job status updates → Real-time communication → GPS check-out confirmation
5

Job Completion & Employer Confirmation

Worker marks job as complete with photos/videos as proof. Employer reviews and confirms job satisfaction.

Confirmation Options: Auto-confirm (after 24 hours) → Manual confirmation → Request revision → Rate worker
6

Commission Calculation

System automatically calculates platform commission, service provider commission (if applicable), and net amount for worker.

Calculations: Gross Amount → Platform Commission → Service Provider Commission → GST (if applicable) → Net Amount
7

Release from Escrow & Distribution

After employer confirmation, amount is released from escrow and automatically distributed to respective wallets.

Distribution: Worker Wallet ← Net Amount | Service Provider Wallet ← Commission (if applicable) | Platform Account ← Platform Commission
8

Invoice Generation & Notification

System generates detailed invoice with breakdown of all charges, commissions, taxes, and GST details.

Invoice Contains: Job details, gross amount, commission breakdown, taxes, GST, net amount, payment method, timestamp
9

Wallet Credit & Fund Availability

Amount is credited to worker's wallet. Worker can immediately use funds for future transactions or request withdrawal.

Wallet Options: Keep in wallet → Use for other services → Request withdrawal → Check transaction history
10

Withdrawal & Bank Transfer

Worker can request withdrawal of wallet balance to their registered bank account or payment method.

Withdrawal Process: Request initiated → KYC verification → Processing (1-3 business days) → Amount transferred to bank account

🔒 Escrow Protection Stages

Secure payment holding system that protects both employer and worker throughout the job lifecycle.

1

Payment Received

Amount collected from employer's payment method and held in secure escrow account.

2

Job In Progress

Amount remains held while worker performs the assigned job. No funds released yet.

3

Job Completed

Worker marks job complete with proof. Employer has 24 hours to confirm satisfaction.

4

Confirmation Period

Employer reviews work quality. Can approve, request revision, or raise dispute.

5

Amount Released

After confirmation, amount is released from escrow and distributed to respective wallets automatically.

6

Dispute Resolution

If dispute raised, admin holds funds and resolves issue. Funds released after resolution.

📊 Commission Management

Platform Commission

  • Default: 10%
  • Per job basis
  • Configurable per category
  • Can be reduced for bulk jobs
  • Real-time calculation

Service Provider Commission

  • Default: 5%
  • Applies when worker assigned through agency
  • Configurable per agency
  • Negotiable contracts
  • Separate wallet tracking

Automatic Deduction

  • Deducted from gross amount
  • Applied at wallet distribution
  • Worker sees net amount only
  • Invoice shows breakdown
  • Fully transparent process

Commission Reports

  • Daily/Monthly analytics
  • Per category reports
  • Per worker analysis
  • Revenue trends
  • Forecasting tools
