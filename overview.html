📋 Platform Overview

Job Boy is a revolutionary on-demand job service platform that connects employers with skilled workers in real-time. By leveraging GPS-based location matching, AI-powered recommendations, and real-time tracking, we enable instant hiring of nearby workers for short-term, location-based services like electrical work, plumbing, delivery, cleaning, and more.

⚡ For Employers

Instantly find and hire nearby workers for urgent jobs. Track progress in real-time, communicate securely, and pay through multiple channels.

💼 For Workers

Get instant job notifications, earn flexible income, build your profile, and grow your business with AI-powered recommendations.

🎯 For Business

Scalable platform with recurring revenue streams through commissions, featured listings, and premium services.

🎯 Market Opportunity

Our Solution

  • Real-time GPS-based worker discovery within minutes
  • Verified, rated workers with KYC verification

  • AI-powered matching and recommendations

💰 Business Model

Revenue Streams

Commission per Job: 10-15% commission on each job booked through the platform
Surge Pricing: Additional charges during peak hours (early morning, evening)
Featured Listings: Workers can pay ₹500-1000/month to be featured prominently
Subscription Plans: Premium plans for workers (₹300/month) and employers (₹500/month)
Service Fees: Processing fees on payments (UPI: 0%, Card: 2.5%, Wallet transfer: 1%)

Financial Model (Year 1)

  • Target Users: 10,000 employers, 5,000 workers
  • Avg Jobs/Month: 5,000 jobs
  • Avg Job Value: ₹2,000
  • Monthly Revenue (Commission): ₹12-18 Lakhs
  • Annual Revenue Projection: ₹1.5-2 Crores
  • Break-even Timeline: 18-24 months

⚠️ Risk Mitigation

Key Risks & Mitigation

  • Low User Adoption: Aggressive marketing, referral rewards
  • Quality Issues: Strict KYC, rating system, dispute resolution
  • Payment Failures: Multiple payment gateways, wallet system
  • Competition: Focus on customer experience and AI differentiation
  • Regulatory Challenges: Compliance-first approach, legal consultation

Quality Assurance

  • KYC verification for all workers (ID + Address proof)
  • Background checks for sensitive service categories
  • Rating system with dispute resolution mechanism
  • Insurance coverage for jobs (future phase)
  • 24/7 customer support with escalation process
